Memory Game

There are 8 pairs of cards, each pair with a different picture, arranged randomly in a 4 by 4 grid. Click on 2 hidden cards flip them. If they match they stay flipped. The game stops when all pairs are matched. Flipping 2 cards counts as one move. You sart with 3 stars and every 3 moves you lose 1 star.
